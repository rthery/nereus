## Standard Formulas Used

### CO2 Table
- Hold time = Personal Best × 50% (normal difficulty)
- 8 rounds, constant hold, decreasing rest
- Rest decrement: 15 seconds per round
- Minimum rest: 60 seconds (normal), 90 seconds (easy), 15 seconds (hard)

### O2 Table
- Max hold = Personal Best × 80% (normal difficulty)
- 8 rounds, increasing hold, constant rest (2:00)
- Hold increment: 15 seconds per round
- Minimum starting hold: 30 seconds

### PB Determination Test
- 2-minute relaxation breathing
- 3 warm-up holds at 50% estimated max, 2-minute rest between
- Final maximum hold attempt
